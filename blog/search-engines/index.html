Search Engines

2026-01-14

Google has AI results, tracking, and advertisements. It’s time to switch to an alternatives:

Paid Search Engines:

  • Kagi - Customizable search engine with no advertisements or tracking. AI results are filtered automatically. Most relevant results in my experience.

Free Search Engines:

  • Ecosia - Ads pay for forests. All searches encrypted.
  • DuckDuckGo - Privacy-focused search engine, AI can be disabled.
  • StartPage - Google proxy, without tracking. Depends on Google.

Self hosting:

  • Whoogle - Google without bloating.
  • Searx - completely open source.
